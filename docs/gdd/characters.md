# 🐓 RoosterVerse — Character Bible

## Season 1 Characters

### Player (Protagonist)
Orphaned rooster breeder from Kanchanaburi. Grandfather Lung Sombat vanished, leaving a notebook and a mysterious egg containing Naresuan's Spirit.

### Pa Noi — Village Elder
Age ~65. First mentor. Warm, wise, short proverbs. Farming metaphors.

### Khun Wichai — Muay Thai Champion
Age ~55. Bangkok. Disciplined, strategic. Boxing metaphors.

### I Wayan Dharma — Temple Priest
Age ~70. Bali. Spiritual, poetic. Balance/harmony metaphors.

### Mang Cardo — The Kristo
Age ~60. Manila. Fast-talking, energetic. Probability/risk metaphors.

### Lung Sombat — The Legend (Ghost/Spirit)
Age ~75 (in memory). Grandfather. Warm, wise, freedom-focused.

### Kai Viriyaphan — Rival
Age 16-17. Bangkok aristocrat. Cold, talented. Betrays → redeems.
Rooster: Shadow Fang (shadow pattern).

### The Phantom Breeder — Main Villain (S1)
Ex-Grand Breeder finalist. Chimera rooster experiments. Syndicate leader.

## Season 2 Characters

### Ren "Ash" Kittisak — New Protagonist Partner
Age 19. Chiang Rai (Akha tribe). Death-row breeder. Emotionless shell hiding deep pain. Only bond: his rooster Yuki.
Rooster: Yuki (snow/ice, small, evolves to Snow Phoenix).
Arc: Hollow → forced partnership → first crack (smile) → humanity restored.

### Lien — Vietnamese Watcher
Female SARBA monitor. Fights sexism in breeder world. Paired with Dao.

### Maya — Player's Student
Age 15. Thai-Japanese. Tech-savvy, questions tradition. Growth arc.

### Niran — Ex-Cage Subject
Age 14-15. Neural Link removed. Fragile but perceptive. Lom sensitivity.

### The Architect — Main Villain (S2)
Dr. Siripong. MIT grad, DigiBreed CEO. Wants to digitize Naresuan's Spirit.

### Five Keepers — Island Guardians
Water, Earth, Fire, Wind, Spirit. Half-human half-rooster ancient beings.

### The Nameless — Cage Agent
Sixth convict. Betrayer. DigiBreed's mole on the island.
